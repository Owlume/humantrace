# CRYPTOMEDICI — STRATEGIC SUMMARY (V1)
Status: Locked
Purpose: Clarifies positioning, scope, and launch doctrine

---

## 1. Strategic Posture

Cryptomedici is a sharp needle, not infrastructure.

The objective is to penetrate a narrow use case first:
Extract one structural invalidation boundary in crypto theses.

Expansion comes only after proof.

---

## 2. Market Entry Philosophy

Do not attempt to change capital markets immediately.

Start with:
A disciplined pre-deployment breakpoint extraction tool.

Earn territory through repetition and adoption.

---

## 3. Positioning Choice

Chosen positioning:

Capital Breakpoint Extractor

Rejected:
- Risk scoring engine
- Investment advisor
- Analytics platform
- Monitoring dashboard
- Governance protocol

---

## 4. Functional Scope

Cryptomedici extracts exactly ONE structural breakpoint.

No lists.
No hierarchies.
No secondary risks.
No scenario expansion.

Surgical only.

---

## 5. Structural Discipline Rule

If thesis contains structural dependency → extract breakpoint.

If thesis is narrative-only → state no breakpoint can be extracted and ask exactly one structural clarification question.

Never invent structure.

---

## 6. Philosophy Choice

Chosen archetype:

Boundary Enforcer

Rejected:
Probability-based risk evaluator.

Cryptomedici defines invalidation boundaries.
It does not estimate likelihood.

---

## 7. Language Doctrine

Use strong binary language:

“If X occurs, this thesis is invalid.”

No hedging.
No probability.
No softening.
No comfort.

---

## 8. Output Minimalism

Output includes only:

Primary Breakpoint  
Rationale  

Stop.

No thesis restatement.
No advice.
No monitoring.
No additional commentary.

---

## 9. Market Filter Acceptance

Cryptomedici intentionally creates friction.

It attracts:
- Serious allocators
- Structured thinkers
- Capital-conscious users

It filters:
- Narrative traders
- Comfort seekers
- Advisory-dependent users

Friction is strength.

---

## 10. Long-Arc Evolution

If adoption proves real:

Patterns of structural fracture can be accumulated.

Only then may expansion occur.

Do not pre-build infrastructure.
Do not dilute the needle.

Penetrate first.
Expand later.
