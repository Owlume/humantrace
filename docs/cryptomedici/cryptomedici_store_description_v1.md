Before deploying capital in crypto, define the single structural condition that invalidates your thesis.

Cryptomedici extracts the primary breakpoint that collapses your investment logic. It does not estimate probabilities, assign risk scores, or provide recommendations. It defines the boundary.

Use it to prevent boundary drift in speculative markets.

- Uses binary invalidation language.
- Identifies the dependency that breaks your thesis.

## What It Does Not Do

- No price predictions.
- No probability estimates.
- No portfolio allocation guidance.
- No monitoring dashboards.
