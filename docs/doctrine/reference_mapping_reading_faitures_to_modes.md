## Purpose of This Document

This document provides a **reference-only mapping** between the canonical
*Reading Failure Types* and Owlume’s existing *Modes* and *Principles*.

It exists to help humans understand conceptual alignment.

It does **not**:
- define runtime behavior,
- introduce inference rules,
- assign weights or priorities,
- permit learning or tuning,
- or create dependencies between doctrine and implementation.

## Mapping Table

### RF01 · Mechanization  
**Core failure:** reducing meaning to explanation

- **Conceptual Mode:** Sensemaking  
- **Principle:** Hold before explaining  
- **Note:** Explanation replaces exposure when meaning is treated as mechanism.

---

### RF02 · Position-Taking  
**Core failure:** early agreement or disagreement

- **Conceptual Mode:** Perspective  
- **Principle:** Suspend stance  
- **Note:** Reading collapses when allegiance precedes understanding.

---

### RF03 · Premature Moralization  
**Core failure:** early moral closure

- **Conceptual Mode:** Judgment Awareness  
- **Principle:** Delay moral collapse  
- **Note:** Moral framing short-circuits narrative development.

---

### RF04 · Linguistic Drift Blindness  
**Core failure:** ignoring language as action

- **Conceptual Mode:** Language Awareness  
- **Principle:** Notice semantic shift  
- **Note:** Meaning changes through wording, not only content.

---

### RF05 · Responsibility Displacement  
**Core failure:** shifting agency away

- **Conceptual Mode:** Agency  
- **Principle:** Return responsibility  
- **Note:** Comfort is preserved by misplacing responsibility.

---

### RF06 · Fairness Projection  
**Core failure:** importing external justice frames

- **Conceptual Mode:** Context  
- **Principle:** Read internal logic first  
- **Note:** External standards override textual coherence.

---

### RF07 · System Substitution  
**Core failure:** abstraction replacing position

- **Conceptual Mode:** Responsibility  
- **Principle:** No abstraction escape  
- **Note:** Systems cannot substitute for situated judgment.

---

### RF08 · Promise Instrumentalization  
**Core failure:** using future assurance to escape present tension

- **Conceptual Mode:** Time Awareness  
- **Principle:** Stay with uncertainty  
- **Note:** Promises become tools instead of commitments.

---

### RF09 · Acceleration Substitution  
**Core failure:** acting to avoid waiting

- **Conceptual Mode:** Process Discipline  
- **Principle:** Do not rush resolution  
- **Note:** Speed replaces fidelity to process.

---

### RF10 · Spiritual / Moral Elitism  
**Core failure:** ranking worth or status

- **Conceptual Mode:** Status Awareness  
- **Principle:** Flatten moral rank  
- **Note:** Hierarchy distorts reading posture.

---

### RF11 · Temporal Compression  
**Core failure:** erasing duration

- **Conceptual Mode:** Narrative Time  
- **Principle:** Honor duration  
- **Note:** Meaning develops through time, not endpoints.

---

### RF12 · Premature Consolation  
**Core failure:** early comfort

- **Conceptual Mode:** Emotional Awareness  
- **Principle:** Do not soothe before seeing  
- **Note:** Consolation replaces exposure.

---

### RF13 · Interpretive Substitution  
**Core failure:** explanation replacing reading

- **Conceptual Mode:** Metacognition  
- **Principle:** Reading before interpretation  
- **Note:** Mastery language displaces attention.

---

### RF14 · Tension Relief Compulsion  
**Core failure:** intolerance of unresolved meaning

- **Conceptual Mode:** Uncertainty Holding  
- **Principle:** Tension is not error  
- **Note:** Some meaning exists only while tension remains.
